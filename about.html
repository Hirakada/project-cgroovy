What is C'Groovy?

At C'Groovy, we believe music isn't just something you hear — it's something you feel. Born from a deep love of rhythm, culture, and connection, C'Groovy is more than a music app — it's your personal soundtrack to life. Join us on a journey where every tap brings you closer to your next favorite song — and maybe even your next favorite memory.

Company Journey

2019 - The Sparks

C’Groovy was born in a cozy creative studio with a bold idea: blend retro charm with modern flair to empower self-expression.

2

2020 - First Drop, First Vibes

Launched our debut capsule collection featuring hand-illustrated designs. Sold out in 3 weeks, gaining traction among indie creatives and urban youth.

2021 - From Local to Lifestyle

Expanded from apparel to lifestyle accessories. Opened our first pop-up booth in a major urban market, receiving media attention and influencer buzz.

3
4

2022 - Collabs & Cultures

Partnered with local musicians, digital artists, and influencers. Hosted our first community-based event, Groove Fest, celebrating art, music, and self-expression.

2023 - Going Digital, Going Global

Launched e-commerce platform with international shipping. Gained our first overseas customers and landed features in online lifestyle magazines.

6

2024 - Sustainability in Style

Introduced eco-conscious packaging and launched a recycled-fabric capsule collection, committing to responsible fashion.

2025 - What's next?

Entering new territories—creative tech, digital collectibles, and immersive brand experiences. The groove never stops.
